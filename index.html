How this is calculated

  1. Take the last 30 stored days for the selected carat and find the high and low.
  2. Position = (current rate − 30-day low) ÷ (30-day high − 30-day low), as a percentage.
  3. Position ≤ 30% → BUY (near the monthly low). Position ≥ 70% → WAIT (near the monthly high). Otherwise → HOLD.
  4. If the 3-day trend is falling and a BUY is already signalled, it's labelled "BUY — dip in progress".
